Kickstarter Campaign

Crowdfunding is a way of raising money for a project where many people put in relatively small amounts of money, usually between one dollar and a few hundred dollars each.
Kickstarter.com is a popular website for crowdfunding campaigns.
We are using Kickstarter to fund development of RPerl v1.0 and beyond.
